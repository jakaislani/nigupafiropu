Patrick Doyle is a Scottish composer. A longtime contributor to actor and director Kenneth Branagh, Doyle is known for his work on films such as Henry V, Sense and Sensibility, Hamlet, Carlito's Way and Gosford Park, as well as Harry Potter and The Goblet of Fire, The Rise of the Planet of the Apes, Thor, Brave, Cinderella and Murder on the Orient Express. Doyle has been nominated for two Oscars and two Golden Globe Awards, and is also the recipient of the ASCAP Henry Mancini Award for "Outstanding Achievement and Contributions to the World of Film and Television Music". Doyle has written for famous directors such as Robert Altman, Ang Lee, Alfonso Cuarón, Mike Newell, Brian De Palma, Chen Kaige, Amma Asante, Régis Wargnier and Kenneth Branagh.
