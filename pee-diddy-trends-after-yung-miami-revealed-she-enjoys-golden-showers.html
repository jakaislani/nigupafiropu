Pee Diddy trends after Yung Miami revealed she enjoys golden showers

Publish date: 2024-08-30

Yung Miami has caused quite a stir on social media with her recent revelation on her podcast “Caresha Please”. During the latest episode, she confessed to Trina that she has a sexual fetish for golden showers, also known as urine play or water sports. This confession immediately sparked a trend on Twitter, with fans quickly making the connection to her partner, Sean “Diddy” Combs, resulting in the hashtag “Pee Diddy” trending within hours of the episode’s release.

Her confession has led to a range of reactions on Twitter, with many users creatively incorporating the “Pee Diddy” theme into their posts. The open dynamics of Miami and Diddy’s relationship have also drawn attention, particularly after Diddy announced the birth of a daughter with another woman in late 2022, but the couple later posted photos from a family yacht trip for New Year’s Day.
