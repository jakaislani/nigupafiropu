Teaser and Storyline of Bosch Legacy Season 2

Hieronymus “Harry” Bosch transitions from the LAPD to a private investigator role in this season, collaborating with defense attorney Honey “Money” Chandler on various cases. Meanwhile, his daughter Maddie embarks on her police journey at Hollywood Station. Bosch delves into investigating businessman Carl Rogers and undertakes a discreet inquiry for billionaire entrepreneur Whitney Vance, unveiling a captivating narrative of post-retirement life, family dynamics, and intriguing cases that beckon him into the investigative realm.
