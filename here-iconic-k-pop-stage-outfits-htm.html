Here are Some of the Most Iconic K-Pop Stage Outfits Ever

Publish date: 2024-08-23

While idols are always seen in amazing outfits, here are some of the most memorable and iconic ones in K-pop!

1. BLACKPINK Jennie's Red Dress

(Photo : News1)
Jennie

For the 8th Gaon Chart Music Awards on January 23, 2019, Jennie wore an iconic red dress for her "SOLO" performance.

2. Red Velvet Joy's Rainbow Dress

(Photo : 인벤)
Joy

During Red Velvet's "Peek-a-boo" promotions, Joy was often seen wearing a spaghetti strap, bodycon rainbow dress.

3. BTS Jimin's Blue Velvet Jacket And Red Scarf

(Photo : COTTON CANDY @cottoncandy__jm on Twitter)
Jimin

4. f(x) Krystal's Tennis Skirt

(Photo : The Klassic (Krsytal.KR))
Krysta

(Photo : EXOonearewe Twitter)
Kai
