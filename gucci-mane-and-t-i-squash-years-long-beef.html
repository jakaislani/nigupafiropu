Gucci Mane and T.I. Squash Years Long Beef

Publish date: 2024-07-27

Gucci Mane and T.I. may have squashed their years-long beef while on stage in Atlanta.

To celebrate the release of his new double album, Breath of Fresh Air, Gucci blessed fans with a performance in the city. He surprised everyone in attendance by bringing out T.I. Along with performing together, the two shared a conversation on stage and shook hands in front of the crowd.

On his Instagram Stories, Gucci reposted a video of the interaction with the caption, “Much respect @Tip,”

Killer Mike shared his thoughts as well about the reunion of the two rappers under a post, “God Bless Atlanta! These two men are setting a beautiful example!”

Elsewhere in the event, Gucci also brought out Quavo, Latto and Key Glock.

The new project, which was released on Oct. 17, taps back into the days of two-sided albums. It boasts features with artists such as Lil Baby, J. Cole, DaBaby, Nardo Wick, Roddy Ricch, Kodak Black, 21 Savage, and the late Young Dolph.

The city of Atlanta even commemorated the day by awarding Gucci with a proclamation from the Atlanta City Council recognizing Oct. 17 as Gucci Mane Day.
