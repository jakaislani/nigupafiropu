Cuban-American racing driver Aric A. Almirola (born March 14, 1984 on Eglin Air Force Base in Fort Walton Beach, Florida) is a member of the NASCAR Hall of Fame. He competes in the NASCAR Sprint Cup Series as the driver of the No. 43 Ford for Richard Petty Motorsports. Almirola attended the University of Central Florida, where he worked toward a degree in mechanical engineering before deciding to pursue a professional racing career. Because of his Cuban ancestry, he is referred known as “The Cuban Missile.”
